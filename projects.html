Kilburn to Marehay Rising Main

Location
Derbyshire
Duration
18 months
Pipework
400–500mm ductile iron
Test Pressure

Installation of 4.5km of ductile iron pipework in highways with permanent tarmac reinstatement. Nuneaton Sewage Rising Mains

Location
Nuneaton, Warwickshire
Duration
6 months
Pipework
400mm & 600mm
Test Pressure
9.5 Bar

Environmentally sensitive installation of 600mm and 400mm new rising mains with online connections to existing mains in a Line-Stop pit. Installed over 1km from the existing pumping station, operating at 850 litres per second. Includes upgrade of roads and new parking areas within the STW.
